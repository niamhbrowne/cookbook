Simply Perfect Beef Spag Bol

Ingredients Needed for this recipe...

  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 16g tomato paste
  • 8ml soy sauce
  • 200g canned tomato frito
  • 11g beef stock mix
  • 5g basil
  • 15ml Henderson's Relish
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 250g British beef mince
  • 190g spaghetti
  • 1 carrot
  • 40g cheddar cheese
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 brown onion

How to cook this dish...

  1. Boil a kettle

    Peel and finely chop the brown onion[s]

    Top, tail, peel and finely chop the carrot
  2. Heat a large, wide-based pan (preferably non-stick) with a drizzle of olive oil over a medium-high heat

    Once hot, add the chopped onion and carrot with a pinch of salt and cook for 3-4 min or until beginning to soften
  3. Peel and finely chop (or grate) the garlic
  4. Once the onion and carrot are beginning to soften, add the beef mince to the pan and increase the heat to high

    Cook for 3-4 min further or until browned all over, breaking it up with a wooden spoon as you go
  5. While the beef mince is cooking, add the spaghetti to a pot of boiled water with a large pinch of salt and bring to the boil over a high heat

    Cook the spaghetti for 8-10 min or until cooked with a slight bite then drain and return to the pot until serving

    Meanwhile, dissolve the chicken stock mix, Henderson’s Relish and soy sauce in 100ml [200ml] boiled water – this is your stock
  6. Add the chopped garlic, tomato paste, stock, tomato frito, dried basil and dried oregano to the pan

    Season with a grind of black pepper, bring to the boil over a high heat and cook for 4-5 min further or until thickened – this is your beef bolognese
  7. Chop the basil finely, including the stalks

    Grate the cheddar cheese

    Serve the beef bolognese over the spaghetti

    Top with the grated cheese and chopped basil and enjoy!
