Tangy Tomato & Goats' Cheese Linguine

Ingredients Needed for this recipe...

  • 190g linguine
  • 1 toasted sesame seed sachet (5g)
  • 120g baby leaf spinach
  • 1/2 tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 1 red wine vinegar sachet (15ml)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 can of finely chopped tomatoes (400g)
  • 1 black sesame seed sachet (5g)
  • 11g veg stock mix
  • 1 75g soft goats cheese

How to cook this dish...

  1. Boil a kettle

    Peel and finely chop (or grate) the garlic
  2. Heat a medium-sized pot with a large drizzle of olive oil over a low heat

    Once hot, add the chopped garlic and chilli flakes (Can't handle the heat? Go easy!)

    Allow them to sizzle in the oil for 30 sec
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and vegetable stock cube[s]

    Increase the heat to medium and stir until the stock cube[s] have dissolved

    Cook for 10-15 min until the tomatoes have reduced to the consistency of a thick sauce
  4. Meanwhile, add the linguine to a large pot of boiled water with a big pinch of salt over a high heat

    Bring to the boil and cook for 8-10 min, or until cooked with a slight bite
  5. Meanwhile, wash, dry, and chop the baby leaf spinach roughly
  6. Add the chopped spinach to the linguine

    Cook for 30 sec until the spinach has wilted, then drain the lot and return to the pot

    Once the tomato sauce has thickened, add the red wine vinegar, 1 tsp [2 tsp] sugar and a generous pinch of salt
  7. Remove the tomato sauce from the heat and stir in half of the goats' cheese, keeping the rest for serving

    Sprinkle the toasted and black sesame seeds onto a chopping board and roll the remaining goats' cheese in the seeds to coat

    Add the tomato and goats' cheese sauce to the pot of cooked linguine and spinach and mix until the linguine is coated

    Serve the linguine with the remaining goats' cheese dotted around

    Enjoy!
